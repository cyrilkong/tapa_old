Privacy Policy

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

TwoPatch Ltd (hereafter “TwoPatch”) collects information on its users when you sign up for an account and when you visit pages on www.twopatch.com. When you register, we will ask you for information about you, including your first name, last name, email address, gender and birth year. Once you register with us, you are not anonymous to us. Page visits may also store information about your computer configuration such as your IP address, cookie information and the pages you requested. TwoPatch may use this information to help make changes to the service to better serve you and our users. We also use the information to customize content on the site that will be displayed to you. TwoPatch will never sell or disclose your information to third parties without your explicit permission.

INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

TwoPatch will never distribute or sell personal information from our users to any third party unaffiliated with TwoPatch except to provide products or services that you have requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;

  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of TwoPatch’s terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

  • We will transfer information about your account to another company if TwoPatch is acquired by or merged. In this event, TwoPatch will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

COOKIES

  • TwoPatch may set and access TwoPatch cookies on your computer.

  • TwoPatch cookies will not store any personal information about you. We use cookies only to recognize you as a user.

ACCOUNT INFORMATION

  • We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the TwoPatch service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and service notifications, that are considered part of your TwoPatch account, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them.

  • You can delete your TwoPatch account by emailing info@twopatch.com and requesting that we delete your account. Any tracking information that may have been collected during your visits to getperx.com may remain after your account has been permanently deleted.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we change our Privacy Policy, we will post the modified privacy policy on this page and revise the “Last Updated” date below. Policy changes will apply to information collected after the date of the change.
